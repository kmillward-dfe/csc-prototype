Glossary

The below information explains some of the terminology used throughout this product in further detail for your understanding.

Term Description
Child in Need (CiN) A Child in Need is a child that has been assessed as needing support because of risks to development or health.
Child Looked After (CLA) A Child Looked After is a child that has been within the care of the Local Authority (LA) for more than 24 hours.
Child Protection Plan (CPP) A Child Protection Plan is made by the Local Authority (LA) for a child's care.
Education Health Care Plan (EHCP) An Education Health Care Plan may be issued following an Education Health Care Assessment. An ECHP is a plan made by the local authority for a child’s special educational provision.
Initial Child Protection Conference (ICPC) An Initial Child Protection Conference can be made as a result of a S47.
Section 47 (S47) A Section 47 enquiry is made where a child is likely to be harmed or suffering and may result in an ICPC.
