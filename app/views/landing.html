Children's Social Care

Overview

Look at data and trends for children’s social care referrals, re-referrals and family assessments. Use this if you work in the children’s social care policy areas at the department for education (DfE).

Where the data comes from

The data comes from local authority children’s social care case management systems and is updated daily, with activity that has been recorded on the child’s case record.

How to use this tool

Get an up-to-date view of performance for each local authority, to help you understand the trends, risks and issues. Use this tool alongside other data sets. The tool shows a child’s journey from referral, assessment, child in need (CiN), child protection and child looked after. Data is updated each day, so may not show the latest information.
